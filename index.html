An Initiative Promoting A Reading Culture

About Us

The Read Reach Initiative is a social enterprise focused on promoting reading culture among youths (young professionals and entrepreneurs) and disadvantaged children. One driving aspiration is to improve the standard of intellect and education in the African community. The initiative is driven towards encouraging reading as a support system for out-of-school children and consequently drive their aspiration for education.
